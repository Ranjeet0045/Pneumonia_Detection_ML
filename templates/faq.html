FAQs – Pneumonia & AI Diagnosis

Is pneumonia contagious? +
Yes. Viral and bacterial pneumonia can spread through respiratory droplets and close contact.
Is AI diagnosis reliable? +
Our model is trained on multiple global datasets and validated against clinical annotations with over 95% recognition accuracy.
Is chest X-ray safe? +
Yes — radiation exposure from a chest X-ray is extremely low and considered medically safe.
Can pneumonia be cured fully? +
With early diagnosis and proper treatment most patients fully recover without long-term effects.
Does COVID-19 cause pneumonia? +
Yes. COVID-19 frequently leads to viral pneumonia visible on chest X-ray imaging.
